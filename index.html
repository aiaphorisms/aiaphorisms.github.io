About Me

Fueled by a deep-seated passion for unraveling the intricate mathematics that underpins learning processes, I am committed to constructing high-impact AI applications that can revolutionize how we approach problems and make decisions. I am equally intrigued by the profound study of medicines and their transformative effects on human biology, always aiming to contribute to the enhancement of both physical and mental health.

My creative spirit finds a canvas in the delicate art of pencil sketching, where I capture moments, express emotions, and bring ideas to life. It's a practice that cultivates my patience and attention to detail, traits that translate well into my professional pursuits.

Beyond my professional and artistic endeavours, I find joy and inspiration in the realm of poetry, literature, and music. These art forms enrich my perspective, infusing creativity into my work and offering respite and rejuvenation in my leisure time.
